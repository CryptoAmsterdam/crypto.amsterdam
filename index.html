We are Crypto Amsterdam.

We believe the next world war goes beyond borders of politics, but targets our digital identity and freedom of speech.
This Trump could be the triumph of many Trumps. And there is no guarantee that any country's future leaders won't abuse today's openness.

Resistance to bad politics isn't done by weapons of destruction. Borders will always move or change, while The Internet will always persist.
Sharing knowledge on secure communication is the only way to guarantee the freedom of this network and borderless world.

We distrust any organization claiming to be trustful with our privacy, and solely rely on tools that are secure by design with end-to-end encryption, and publicly reviewable source code.
It is our mission to make safe communication understood by everyone, and make the concepts just as simple part of our day as locking our house doors.
We show you the right tools, without sacrificing usability. With the ultimate dream to make encryption part of the elementary school's curriculum.

With everyone connected securely, we can continue the freedom of speech and knowledge the internet has brought us.
We are the resistance,
we are crypto.amsterdam.
