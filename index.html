We are Crypto Amsterdam.

You are constantly being watched. All your online activities are being monitored. It targets our digital identity and freedom of speech.
Sharing knowledge on secure and private communication is the only way to guarantee freedom in this networked and borderless world.

It is our mission to make safe communication understood by everyone, and make the concepts just as simple as locking our doors.

We solely rely on tools that are secure by design, protect our privacy and are released under an open and liberal license.
